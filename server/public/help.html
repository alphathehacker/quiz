Help & User Guide

🎓 Student Instructions

  • Go to the Login page and select the Student role.
  • Login using your student credentials (e.g., student@quiz.com).
  • Access your personalized Student Dashboard.
  • Participate in live and past quizzes.
  • View scores and history of attempts.

👩‍🏫 Faculty Instructions

  • Go to the Login page and select the Faculty role.
  • Login using your faculty credentials (e.g., faculty@quiz.com).
  • Manage quizzes via your Faculty Dashboard.
  • Create new quizzes and manage quiz questions.
  • Monitor student performance and quiz analytics.

🛠 Common Issues & Solutions

  • Forgot your password? Contact your faculty/admin to reset it.
  • Quiz not accessible? Ensure it's published and active.
  • Login problems? Double-check the role selection (Student/Faculty).
